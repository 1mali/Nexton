Redefining the art of serviced accommodation

Who we are

Based in the heart of London, we specialise in providing luxury mid-term to long-term stays that combine comfort, elegance, and convenience. Our mission is to create an exceptional living experience for professionals, expatriates, and travellers who seek more than just a place to stay—they seek a true home away from home.

At Nexton, we understand that a luxurious stay is about more than just aesthetics; it’s about providing an environment where you can relax and thrive. Our dedicated team is committed to delivering unparalleled service and support, ensuring that every aspect of your stay is handled with the utmost care and attention. Whether you're here for a few months or a year, we strive to make your experience as enjoyable and hassle-free as possible.

We focus on transforming unique properties into premium serviced accommodations that offer exceptional luxury and comfort. We are always on the lookout for distinctive properties that align with our high standards of quality and elegance.
